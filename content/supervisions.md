Master theses should aim for the [ETH Medal](https://ethz.ch/content/dam/ethz/common/docs/weisungssammlung/files-de/preis-master-arbeiten.pdf).

Bachelor, Semester, and Master theses should also aim for the [PBL Best Thesis Award](https://ee.ethz.ch/news-and-events/d-itet-news-channel/2025/10/at-pbl-open-day-six-outstanding-students-honoured.html).

To be eligible for these awards, you need a minimum grade of 5.5 for the PBL Awards or a 6 for the ETH Medal. Achieving a grade of 6 is evaluated based on the following criteria:
- Project difficulty and complexity
- Student engagement, i.e., consistent dedication and effort throughout the project
- Significant improvement over the course of the project
- Major contributions made independently, beyond the assigned tasks
- Exceptional approach and methodology, exceeding expectations
- High-quality results or breakthrough findings that can be used in further research
- Excellent documentation, serving as a strong example of report writing
- Outstanding presentation skills
- Active involvement in preparing a scientific paper
